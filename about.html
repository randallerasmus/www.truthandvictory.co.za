Welcome to Truth and Victory Since 2019

She was advised not to do so,but because there were hundreds of children , adults and pensioners in her area daily before her eyes struggling to make ends meet. And because I was not fortunate to do so I shared my little with whoever I can until a A certain individuals and a group came and woke up my desire to feed our starving children in my community in the Christmas of 2019. From there I continued and stood firm and when lockdown started in 2020 the need for food became more and more.People lost more jobs more kids went to bed hungry and i perservered and succeed to register the npo Truth and Victory in the face of all adversary.
